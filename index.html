Experience

Since I didn't know what to write in this section, I'll describe my experience with Adobe Animate. Only a woman giving birth can roughly understand the pain experienced by a student working at Adobe Animate. But seriously, from the first task, it was not very clear why we use this program, because its functionality is the same as, for example, Adobe After Effects, but a little more inconvenient. Sometimes it seemed that it would be more convenient to draw each frame by hand than to work in this program. Of course, I found it a little interesting to work with the forms and symbols in tasks 3, 4 and 5, but it seems to me that it was just the Stockholm syndrome that began to manifest itself. While working on task 6, I made sure that it was Stockholm syndrome. Only by catching strong inspiration you can work with this wonderful program. But it seems that by the 8th task it turned out to cope with this chimera at least a little
